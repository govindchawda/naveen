About Us

Synergic Crop Solution is a leading agrochemical company in the country that manufactures and markets several well-known brands. The company is committed to strengthening Indian agriculture with a productive and progressive vision and mission to support the prosperity of Indian farmers. With a long-term vision of enhancing productivity and improving the standard of living of Indian farmers, Synergic Crop Solution aims for organic and inorganic growth in India and overseas in agrochemicals, seeds, fertilizers, and chemicals. The company emphasizes sustainability and profitability in every business unit.

OUR PURPOSE

We work towards a safer and healthier planet by leveraging our research capabilities and bringing sustainable, safe, technological solutions to consumers globally.

OUR VALUES

Ethics : We conduct ourselves and our business affairs in accordance with the highest ethical standards.

Quality : We take responsibility for quality. Our product and services will be amongst the best in terms of value delivered. Each of us is responsible for the quality of whatever we do.

Long term : We base our decisions on maximizing the long term success of our company.

Customer First : We will work towards a customer focused organization, keeping the customer front at center in all we do.

Ownership : Each individual doing what it takes to win.
